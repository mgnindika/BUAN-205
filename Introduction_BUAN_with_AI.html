Types of Analytics: Definitions & Use Cases

‚ñº

Descriptive: analyzes historical data using aggregation and data mining to uncover trends and patterns; common in business reporting such as revenue summaries, inventory reports and dashboards like Google Analytics.

Predictive: leverages statistical models and machine learning to estimate future outcomes. It powers fraud detection, marketing optimization and risk scoring, and even deep‚Äëlearning diagnostics in medicine.

Prescriptive: goes beyond prediction to recommend the best course of action by simulating scenarios. It‚Äôs used in healthcare to optimize treatment plans and in finance to set pricing strategies.
