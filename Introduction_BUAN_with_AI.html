What is Business Analytics?

BA is the use of **data**, **technology**, **statistical analysis**, and **quantitative methods** to gain insights and make better, fact-based decisions.

It's the intersection of **business**, **computing**, and **data**.

The 3 Pillars of BA

(This chart is a conceptual representation of the core pillars.)

The Analytics Journey

1. Data → Information

Taking raw data and giving it context (e.g., this number is a sales figure).

2. Information → Insight

Discovering meaningful patterns (e.g., sales are higher in the Asia Pacific region).

3. Insight → Action

Making a decision based on the insight (e.g., launching a new marketing campaign in Asia Pacific).

The Three Types of Analytics

Descriptive Analytics

**What happened?** Summarizes past and current data.

Predictive Analytics

**What will happen?** Forecasts future trends based on past data.

Prescriptive Analytics

**What should we do?** Recommends an optimal course of action.
